The Spans of Life

Canon lifespans compared — bar = the normal span; diamond = the recorded extreme. The Eldar's bar runs off the scale: within Arda they do not die of age at all.

Reading notes. Solid segments join attested or model anchor points; nothing is interpolated between eras. Númenor's line ends at the Downfall (S.A. 3319) — the island population was annihilated; survivors continue in the Dúnedain-realms line. Ents have had zero births since the Second Age. Easterlings and Haradrim appear in DEMOGRAPHY.md only as army-derived lower bounds (≥10⁶ collectively) and are not charted. Confidence: H = Tolkien's own figure; M = anchored inference; L = order-of-magnitude. Full method — six labeled estimators M1–M6 — in DEMOGRAPHY.md §2. External information: the kindred-counts 14/56/74 were cross-checked against external indices of NoME and confirmed against the corpus text; no external figure overrides the corpus anywhere.
