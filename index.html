The Law of the Archive

The twenty-eight books of the corpus are the only authority. Every statement carries its source; estimates carry their method and confidence — C canon I inferred (H/M/L) EXT external, fenced NEO not canon — and where the books are silent, the archive says exactly so: "The attached documents do not provide enough information to answer this." External maps, lexicons and scholarship were studied and credited, and never allowed to override a line of Tolkien's own. Contradictions the Professor left us — two Valian-year lengths, Gil-galad's fathers, Amrod's two deaths, the Angerthas value-shifts — are displayed, not resolved.
