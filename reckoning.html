The coinage of Gondor

'In Gondor tharni was used for a silver coin, the fourth part of the castar (in Noldorin the canath or fourth part of the mirian).' — PM. Bree reckoned in silver pennies: a pony was worth ~4 (Bill Ferny demanded 12, 'at least three times the pony's value'). No exchange between castar and penny is given: the attached documents do not provide enough information to answer this.
