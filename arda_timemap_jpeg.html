The Living Map

❦ ✦ ❦

The map itself is untouched — drowned Beleriand joined to the lands of the Third Age. Everything upon it is an overlay you command:

Borders — slide the age-bar below the map through thirteen slices of history, Years of the Trees to the Fourth Age; realm outlines and the era's events follow.
Places — 91 sites, each with its story and citation; toggle elvish names for the other tongue where canon gives one.
⚔ Battles — 23 fields; click through to the animated battle-maps of the armies page.
Journeys — ten roads: Tuor's, Beren's and Túrin's through Beleriand, the Quest of Erebor, and the six roads of the War of the Ring, day-stamped from the Tale of Years; scrub the day-bar or press ride.
📏 Measure — click two points for the distance in miles (scale calibrated on Hobbiton–Rivendell, [I]-labeled).

Sources: the corpus throughout (App A/B, UT, Silm, the Great Tales; Fonstad's Atlas as corpus cross-check). The interaction patterns follow the great fan cartographies — LotRProject's journeys-with-dates, Arda Maps' age-slices, ArdaCraft's path-quotes, 3rin.gs' dual naming — rebuilt here corpus-first; External information note in ARDA_TIMELINE.md.
