Imladris (Rivendell)

refuge-lordship · Elves (mixed), wardens of the North · S.A. 1697 – F.o.A.

Not a kingdom: Elrond as master and healer, ruling by wisdom — 'his house was perfect, whether you liked food, or sleep, or work, or story-telling, or singing, or just sitting and thinking best'; the Council its instrument of policy, summoning none yet gathering all.
