The Annals of the Archive

what has been wrought, and when — every shipped update to the Arda Archive, newest first
2026-07-23The great polish. Sitewide overhaul: grouped navigation with plain-word subtitles and breadcrumbs, keyboard access and visible focus for every control, dark theme (☾ in the nav), readable citations, page descriptions and previews for sharing, crawlable pages for every realm, person and place, a sitemap and a proper 404, faster map loading, a fixed update-delivery bug (returning visitors now always get the newest archive), and these Annals.
2026-07-23The Realms of Arda. The political atlas: 60 polities from Valinor to the Reunited Kingdom with cited dossiers of government, law, economy, war, diplomacy, tongue and story; borders through thirteen slices of time; the Rise & Fall of every state on one strip.
2026-07-23The Reckonings of Arda. Every calendar and measure: Shire Reckoning to the yén, a date-converter across all reckonings and into our calendar (exact where Tolkien is exact), feast-days of every people, the ranga, the lár and the coinage of Gondor.
2026-07-22The Re-ingestion. All 28 volumes re-extracted (5.6M words), a corpus structure index, a 541-entity concordance ("In the books" panels on every record), an 18-item classified contradiction register, and a 521-date machine audit with conservative fixes.
2026-07-22The proposals completed. Offline app (PWA), Side by Side comparisons, and The Silences — the corpus's deepest gaps gathered and left honestly unfilled.
2026-07-22The Turning of the Ages & Spoken Arda. Map and chronicle joined on one slider through 21,000 years; every name speakable aloud by Appendix E's rules.
2026-07-22Place records & guided tours. A record page for every place, joining battles, borders, journeys and treasures; six narrated tours with the living halls beside the story.
2026-07-21The Gallery. Original armour-and-weapon panoplies and great places, drawn to the Professor's own words, film-design tradition credited as staging only.
2026-07-21Permanence. The archive deployed to its lasting home at samsikallo.github.io/arda-archive, with a self-contained offline copy.
2026-07-21Verse, Trial, touch. 35 poems letter-for-letter; the Trial of Lore quiz drawn live from the archive's own data; full phone and tablet support.
2026-07-21Artifacts & journeys. Twenty treasures with full chains of custody; the journeys of Tuor, Beren and Túrin ride the living map.
2026-07-20Search, deep links, records. Universal search over every hall; a shareable link to everything; assembled record pages for every person.
2026-07-19The great reworks. Genealogy, peoples, armies (animated battle-maps), timeline, the Living Map, and the tongues & letters hall rebuilt to full depth, corpus-checked line by line.
