The Named Blades — an armoury of legend

Women and war [the canon register]. Every attested field army of the corpus is male, with three cited exceptions kept visible rather than converted into invented percentages: the nissi of the Eldar, who "fought valiantly in dire straits or desperate defence" (HoME X, Laws & Customs); Haleth's "picked bodyguard of women" (UT, The Drúedain) — the one institutional female unit; and Éowyn at the Pelennor, the exception that proves the Rohirric norm. Sieges evacuate "women and children" (Helm's caves, Minas Tirith). Estimation methods M1–M8 and all ranges: ARMIES.md.
